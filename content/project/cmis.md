Title:     What is CMIS?

# What is CMIS?

## About CMIS

[CMIS (Content Management Interoperability Services)][0] is an OASIS
standard enabling information sharing between different Content 
Management Systems. 
 
See also: 

  - [Wikipedia][1]
  - [OASIS][2]
  - [CMIS Specification][0]
